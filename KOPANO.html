ABOUT US

Telkom is a South African telecommunications company that provides a range of ICT services, including voice, broadband, and internet, as well as mobile and data center solutions. It is the largest fixed broadband operator and converged communications provider in South Africa, measured by its mobile network. Telkom is majority state-owned and operates across the African continent

OUR SERVICES

Telkom SA SOC Limited is a South African wireline and wireless telecommunications provider, operating in more than 38 countries across the African continent. Telkom is majority state-owned (55.3%) with the South African government owning 40.5% of Telkom, while another 14.8% is owned by another state-owned company - the Public Investment Corporation (PIC), which is closely linked to the South African government.>

Telkom provides connectivity through ducted and aerial copper, fibre, satellite, mobile and wireless transmission.
